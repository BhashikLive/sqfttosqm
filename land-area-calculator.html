Understanding Land Measurements

Land in India is measured in multiple units depending on the region:

  • North India: Bigha, Gaj (square yards), Square feet
  • South India: Cent, Ground, Square feet, Square meters
  • West India: Guntha, Acre, Square meters
  • East India: Katha, Decimal, Bigha

How to measure irregular plot area?

For irregular plots, divide the land into rectangles or triangles, calculate each area separately, then add them together. For accurate measurement, hire a licensed surveyor who can use GPS and theodolite equipment.

1 guntha = 1,089 square feet or 101.17 square meters. Commonly used in Maharashtra and Karnataka. 40 gunthas = 1 acre. A typical residential plot is 4-6 gunthas in these states.
