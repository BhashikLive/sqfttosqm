Square Feet to Square Meters Conversion Formula

sqm = sqft × 0.092903

Or alternatively:

sqm = sqft ÷ 10.764

To convert square feet (sqft) to square meters (sqm), multiply the area value in square feet by 0.092903. This is the standard conversion factor recognized internationally.

Real Estate & Construction Insights

Is 1,000 sqft big?

A 1,000 square-foot home equals roughly 92.9 square meters. That comfortably fits two bedrooms, a kitchen, and a living room. In European listings you’ll see the same footprint advertised as a 93 sqm flat.

Common flooring orders

Flooring is usually sold in square meters internationally. If a contractor quotes 120 sqm of tile, that’s 1,291 sqft—knowing both numbers avoids over-ordering.

Land measurement equivalents

One acre is 43,560 sqft or 4,046.86 sqm. For smaller plots, 5 marla equals about 1,361 sqft (126.4 sqm), which helps when comparing regional listings.

About Square Feet and Square Meters

Square Feet (sqft)

A square foot is a unit of area used primarily in the United States, United Kingdom, Canada, and other countries. One square foot is equal to the area of a square with sides measuring one foot (12 inches) in length.

Square Meters (sqm)

A square meter is the SI unit of area measurement used worldwide. One square meter equals the area of a square with sides measuring one meter in length. It's the standard unit in the metric system.

Area Measurements in India, UK, and US

Area Units in India

In India, both imperial and metric units are widely used. Residential properties are commonly listed in square feet (sqft) or square yards (gaj), especially in North India. Square meters (sqm) are standard in architect plans and government approvals. Regional units like bigha, guntha, and cents are also common for land measurements.

Common conversions for India:

  • 1 gaj (square yard) = 9 square feet = 0.836 square meters
  • 100 gaj = 900 sqft = 83.6 sqm (typical 2BHK)
  • 1 bigha ≈ 27,225 sqft ≈ 2,529 sqm (varies by state)
  • 1 guntha = 1,089 sqft ≈ 101.2 sqm (Maharashtra, Karnataka)

Area Units in United Kingdom

The UK has transitioned to metric measurements for property. Modern listings show square meters (sqm), but many buyers still think in square feet. Estate agents often provide both measurements.

UK property sizes:

  • Small flat: 40-50 sqm (430-540 sqft)
  • 2-bedroom flat: 60-80 sqm (645-860 sqft)
  • 3-bedroom house: 100-130 sqm (1,076-1,400 sqft)

Area Units in United States

The United States exclusively uses square feet for property listings. Square meters are rarely seen in residential real estate, though they may appear in international architectural plans.

US typical sizes:

  • Studio apartment: 400-600 sqft (37-56 sqm)
  • 1-bedroom: 700-900 sqft (65-84 sqm)
  • 3-bedroom house: 1,500-2,500 sqft (139-232 sqm)
  • Residential lot: 5,000-10,000 sqft (0.11-0.23 acres)
